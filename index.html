Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland is a animated musical produced by Walt Disney, It was released in 1951. It is based on Lewis Carroll's 1865 novel "Alice's Adventure in Wonderland". The film is about a young girl called Alice, who falls down a rabbit hole and enters a whimsical world. Wonderland, which is ruled by none other than the Queen of Hearts. Alice has also encountered new friends such as the Mad Hatter and Cheshire cat.
